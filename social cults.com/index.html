Outstanding approach

You might wonder how we make the magic happen from Vancouver, how our digital marketing agency carves out success in the competitive digital landscape.

  • QUESTIONNAIRE
    Completing a questionnaire (brief) is a crucial step in client proposal preparation. Writing a digital marketing brief is essential to aligning your marketing goals with us, as well as all stakeholders involved in the business. It acts as a blueprint providing you with the necessary structure to achieve your desired strategic digital marketing goals
    1
  • GOALS
    Digital marketing objectives underpin all marketing strategies. Setting marketing goals should always be the priority as they will be the basis of your entire strategy. Without clear goals, you're basically directionless. The range of marketing objectives you can create are many and varied
    2
  • ESTIMATION
    After we have figured out the details of your business and you have set your goals, we can evaluate the necessary efforts to achieve the result. An estimation encompasses strategies, timelines, channels, and budget. Based on our objectives (attraction, conversion, and loyalty), we'll start to carry out different strategies
    3
  • ANALYSIS
    The first thing you need to do when developing your digital marketing plan is to carry out an internal and external analysis (SWOT analysis) of the company. A useful framework for this is the SWOT analysis that allows you to look at the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats for your company and the market at large.
    4
  • STRATEGY
    A digital marketing strategy is a plan for establishing an internet presence through online channels such as organic search, social media, paid ads, and other web-based mediums such as your website. The 5 stages of strategic digital marketing planning include plan, reach, act, convert and engage
    5
  • PROCESS
    This is the key stage, which proves why we're different. We treat each project as if it were our only or last client that would determine our agency's future. We believe that one is just as cool as its result. As of April 2023, our client ranks within Google TOP-3 for 150+ keywords in 35 countries within the web development niche.
    6
  • DELIVERY
    We deliver all services to customers on time and always meet the KPIs. We are able to do so because of our process: filling out the questionnaire, asking you to set clear goals, correctly estimating the time and budget, conducting a market analysis, developing a strategy, and adhering to it.
    7
  • FINAL EDITS
    We are perfectionists. We believe that no matter how hard we try, the result could always be better.
    If the ideas are realistic, we will implement those ideas (still this doesn't always happen, since it is unlikely that NASA will approve your banner on the Mars rover even though it is certainly a cool idea).
    8
  • REPORTING
    We all have a need to visualize and understand the fruits of our labours. Primarily, this is so we can optimize campaigns, see what is working (or not!) and give ourselves the knowledge and insight. The other side of the coin is that there are, invariably, other stakeholders that will need to see that insight.
